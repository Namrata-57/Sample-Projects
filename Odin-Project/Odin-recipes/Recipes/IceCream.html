How to make a Ice Cream



Description

Strawberry ice cream is super easy to make with this recipe that doesn't use an egg-custard base but still tastes rich and creamy.

This strawberry ice cream is rich, creamy, and full of fresh and fruity flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mashed fresh strawberries
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 cups heavy Cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ¬º teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup white Sugar
  • 2 drops red Food coloring(optional)

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Combine strawberries, milk, cream, sugar, vanilla, salt, and food coloring in a large bowl.

  3. Pour strawberry mixture into the freezer bowl of an ice cream maker; freeze according to the manufacturer's directions.

  4. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze until firm.
